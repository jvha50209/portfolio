kansas city odense

website redesign project

For our first major project on first semester of multimedia design we were tasked with redesigning an existing website and presenting it as a hi-fi prototype in Adobe XD. We were divided into small groups who each chose a site to work on. My group chose Kansas City Odense, and I worked on redesigning the front page. Here is a link to our Adobe XD prototype.

woofie – dog dating app

mock mobile app prototype

As part of a workshop during first semester of multimedia design we were divided into groups, each tasked with conceptualizing as well as creating a hi-fi prototype for a dating app. Our group chose dog owners as our target audience, and we came up with Woofie – a dog dating app. I mainly worked on designing the dog profile page. Here is a link to our Adobe XD prototype.

batavus verona ego

mock ad campaign

Back when I was studying to become a media graphic, one of our major assignments were to design an entire (mock) ad campaign in connection with an upcoming advertising promotion from the retailer Fri BikeShop for an electric bike – the Batavus Verona EGo. I designed a logo, an abribus, a bus ad, a brochure, a presentation box for the keys as well as a landing page promoting the bike.

logo design
